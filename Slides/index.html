Outage Monitoring

StatusSphere

Consolidated visibility into service outages across major financial institutions and cloud service providers.

Team: Heartbeat Hunters Charles Lim, Bruce Wang, Jensen Lim, Aman Singhal — TCRD
The Problem

Fragmented Manual Monitoring

  • Supervisors check individual bank websites every minute during incidents
  • No centralized dashboard for 1-2 monthly outage incidents
  • Cannot quickly determine which banks are affected during CSP outages
  • Delayed situational awareness hampers regulatory response
Users: TCRD & FSG officers (primary), TG & Senior Management (situational awareness)
The Solution

StatusSphere Dashboard

  • Automated aggregation of service status from multiple banks
  • Real-time outage information, recovery progress, and news
  • Faster and more accurate outage detection
  • Consolidated situational awareness in a single interface
