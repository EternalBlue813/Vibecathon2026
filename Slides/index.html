Outage Monitoring

StatusSphere

Consolidated visibility into service outages across major financial institutions and cloud service providers.

Team: Heartbeat Hunters Charles Lim, Bruce Wang, Jensen Lim, Aman Singhal — TCRD
The Problem

Fragmented Manual Monitoring

  • Supervisors check individual bank websites every minute during incidents
  • No centralized dashboard for outage incidents
  • Community-driven sites such as downdetector do not offer information on bank outage banners and a dashboard with preferred entities grouped together
  • Cannot quickly determine which banks are affected during CSP outages
  • Delayed situational awareness hampers regulatory response
The Solution

StatusSphere Dashboard

  • Automated aggregation of service status from multiple banks
  • Real-time outage screenshots including web banner information and recovery progress
  • Related news coverage included for comprehensive context
  • LLM enabled to collate and summarize unstructured information, provide answers to questions
  • Consolidated situational awareness in a single interface
Target Users
  • Primarily for TCRD & FSG officers' supervisory work
  • TG & Senior Management for situation awareness
